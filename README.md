###  Retail Performance Dashboard (Power BI)
Analyzed ~5,000 retail orders to understand sales trends, customer behavior, product performance and shipping efficiency.

**Key Insights:**
- Sales peak in December and drop in January
- High discounts reduce profit without boosting sales
- Copiers are the most profitable subcategory
- Standard Class shipping is the most profitable delivery method

###  Coffee Sales Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)
Analyzed coffee sales transactions from 2019–2022 to understand revenue trends, customer behavior and product profitability.

**Key Insights:**
- Total sales of $45,134 across ~1,000 orders and 913 unique customers
- Sales are heavily concentrated in the United States
- Larger product sizes generate the highest profit
- Profit dips around August each year
